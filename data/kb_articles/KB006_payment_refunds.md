# KB006 — Payment Methods & Refund Timelines

**doc_id**: KB006  
**category**: payments  
**last_updated**: 2026-04-01

## Accepted Payment Methods

- Credit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, RuPay)
- Debit Cards
- UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm, BHIM)
- Net Banking (all major banks)
- EMI (credit card EMI, No-Cost EMI on select products)
- Buy Now Pay Later (BNPL) — ZestMoney, LazyPay
- Cash on Delivery (COD) — available on orders up to ₹10,000
- Store Wallet / Gift Cards

## No-Cost EMI

Available on select products above ₹3,000. Tenure options: 3, 6, 9, 12 months. Bank processing fee waived by the seller as a discount.

## Refund Timelines by Payment Method

| Payment Method | Refund Timeline |
|---|---|
| Credit Card | 5–7 business days |
| Debit Card | 5–7 business days |
| UPI | 2–3 business days |
| Net Banking | 3–5 business days |
| Store Wallet | Within 24 hours |
| COD | NA (no payment made) |
| BNPL | 5–7 business days (loan reversed) |

## Failed Payment

If your payment fails but amount is debited:

- Most cases auto-reversed within **3–5 business days**.
- If not reversed, raise a complaint via **My Account → Payment Issues** with your bank transaction reference.
- Our team resolves within **5 business days**.

## Invoice & GST

- GST invoice generated automatically for every order.
- Available for download from **My Orders → Download Invoice**.
- Business customers can add GSTIN at checkout for B2B invoicing.

## COD Charges

Cash on Delivery attracts a convenience fee of **₹29 per order**, non-refundable.
