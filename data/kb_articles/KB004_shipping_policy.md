# KB004 — Shipping & Delivery Policy

**doc_id**: KB004  
**category**: shipping  
**last_updated**: 2026-04-01

## Delivery Timelines

| Shipping Type | Estimated Delivery |
|---|---|
| Standard Delivery | 4–7 business days |
| Express Delivery | 1–2 business days |
| Same-Day Delivery (select cities) | Order before 11 AM |
| Remote / Rural Areas | 7–14 business days |

## Shipping Charges

- **Free standard shipping** on orders above ₹499
- Express delivery: ₹99–₹149 depending on weight and location
- Same-day delivery: ₹199 flat fee

## Tracking Your Order

1. Once shipped, you will receive an SMS and email with a **tracking number**.
2. Track your order via **My Orders** → **Track Shipment**, or directly on the courier partner's website.
3. Tracking updates every 4–6 hours.

## Delivery Failure

If delivery fails (customer unavailable, incorrect address):

- Courier will attempt delivery **2 more times** on subsequent days.
- After 3 failed attempts, the package is returned to the warehouse.
- A full refund is issued within 5–7 business days, excluding original shipping charges.

## Damaged During Shipping

If the package is visibly damaged upon arrival:

- **Do not accept** the delivery — ask the courier to mark it as "refused due to damage".
- If accepted and damage found inside, report within **48 hours** via My Orders → Report Issue.
- Replacement dispatched within 2–3 business days after verification.

## Address Change After Order

Address changes are allowed **only before the order is shipped**. Once shipped, address cannot be modified. Contact support immediately if needed.
