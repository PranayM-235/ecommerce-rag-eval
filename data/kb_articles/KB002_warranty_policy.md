# KB002 — Warranty Policy

**doc_id**: KB002  
**category**: warranty  
**last_updated**: 2026-04-01

## Standard Warranty Coverage

All electronics sold on our platform come with a **manufacturer warranty**. The warranty period varies by product category:

| Category | Warranty Period |
|---|---|
| Smartphones | 12 months |
| Laptops & Computers | 12 months |
| Televisions | 24 months |
| Audio Devices (headphones, speakers) | 12 months |
| Home Appliances (large) | 24 months |
| Home Appliances (small) | 12 months |
| Accessories & Peripherals | 6 months |

## What is Covered

- Manufacturing defects
- Hardware failures under normal usage
- Battery defects (capacity drop below 80% within warranty period)
- Screen defects not caused by physical damage

## What is NOT Covered

- Physical damage (cracked screens, dents, liquid damage)
- Damage caused by unauthorised repair or modification
- Normal wear and tear
- Consumables (batteries after warranty, ink cartridges, etc.)
- Damage caused by use outside the product's intended purpose

## How to Claim Warranty

1. Visit **My Orders** → select the product → click **Raise Warranty Claim**.
2. Describe the issue and attach photos/videos of the defect.
3. Our team will review within **2 business days**.
4. Approved claims: product picked up for inspection at no cost to the customer.
5. Resolution time: **7–14 business days** from pickup.

## Extended Warranty (Optional Purchase)

Customers may purchase an **Extended Warranty Plan** at checkout:

- 1-year extension: ₹499–₹1,999 depending on product price
- 2-year extension: ₹799–₹2,999
- Covers accidental damage (screen cracks, liquid spills) in addition to manufacturing defects

## Warranty on Refurbished Products

Refurbished products sold under our **Renewed** programme carry a **6-month seller warranty**, not a manufacturer warranty.
