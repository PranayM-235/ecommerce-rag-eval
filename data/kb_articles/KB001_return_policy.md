# KB001 — Return Policy

**doc_id**: KB001  
**category**: returns  
**last_updated**: 2026-04-01

## Standard Return Window

Customers may return most items within **30 days** of the purchase date for a full refund. The item must be:

- Unused and in its original packaging
- Accompanied by the original receipt or order confirmation email
- Returned with all accessories, manuals, and bundled items

## Non-Returnable Items

The following items **cannot** be returned under any circumstances:

- Digital downloads and software licenses (once activated)
- Perishable goods (food, flowers, etc.)
- Personalised or custom-engraved items
- Intimate apparel and swimwear (for hygiene reasons)
- Hazardous materials and flammable liquids

## Extended Return Window — Festive Season

During the festive season (November 1 to January 15), the return window is extended to **60 days** for all eligible items. 

## How to Initiate a Return

1. Log in to your account and go to **My Orders**.
2. Select the item you wish to return and click **Request Return**.
3. Choose the reason for return and upload a photo if the item is damaged.
4. Print the prepaid return shipping label and drop the package at any authorised courier point.
5. Refund will be processed within **5–7 business days** of the warehouse receiving the item.

## Refund Method

- Original payment method (credit/debit card, UPI, net banking): 5–7 business days
- Store credit / wallet: within 24 hours of return approval

## Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item, you are eligible for a **free replacement or full refund** regardless of the 30-day window, provided the damage is reported within **48 hours** of delivery.
