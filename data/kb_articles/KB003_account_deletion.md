# KB003 — Account Deletion & GDPR / Data Privacy

**doc_id**: KB003  
**category**: account  
**last_updated**: 2026-04-01

## Account Deletion Request

Customers have the right to permanently delete their account at any time. Once deleted:

- All personal data is removed from active systems within **30 days**
- Order history is anonymised and retained for **7 years** for legal/tax compliance (GST, income tax records)
- Wallet balance must be withdrawn before deletion — unused balance is forfeited after account closure

## How to Delete Your Account

1. Go to **Account Settings** → **Privacy** → **Delete My Account**.
2. You will be asked to confirm your identity via OTP.
3. Any active orders must be delivered or cancelled before deletion can proceed.
4. Submit your deletion request.
5. You will receive a confirmation email within **24 hours**.
6. Full data deletion is completed within **30 days**.

## GDPR / DPDP Compliance

Under the **Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP)** and applicable international regulations (GDPR for EU customers):

- You have the right to **access** your personal data (submit a Data Access Request).
- You have the right to **correction** of inaccurate data.
- You have the right to **erasure** ("right to be forgotten") — fulfilled via account deletion.
- You have the right to **data portability** — export your data from Account Settings → Privacy → Download My Data.

**Data Access / Portability Request processing time**: 15 business days.

## Account Suspension vs Deletion

| | Suspension | Deletion |
|---|---|---|
| Reversible? | Yes, within 90 days | No — permanent |
| Data retained? | Yes, fully | Anonymised after 30 days |
| Triggered by | Policy violation / fraud | Customer request |

## Reactivation

A suspended account can be reactivated by contacting support within 90 days. A deleted account **cannot** be reactivated under any circumstances.
