# KB005 — Order Cancellation Policy

**doc_id**: KB005  
**category**: cancellation  
**last_updated**: 2026-04-01

## When Can You Cancel?

| Order Status | Cancellable? |
|---|---|
| Order Placed (processing) | Yes — instant cancellation |
| Payment Pending | Yes — auto-cancelled if unpaid after 30 min |
| Shipped | No — initiate return after delivery |
| Out for Delivery | No |
| Delivered | No — initiate return |

## How to Cancel

1. Go to **My Orders** → select the order → click **Cancel Order**.
2. Choose cancellation reason.
3. Confirm cancellation.
4. Refund initiated immediately for prepaid orders.

## Refund on Cancellation

- **Prepaid orders**: Full refund to original payment method within 5–7 business days, or store credit within 24 hours.
- **COD orders**: No payment taken, so no refund needed.

## Partial Cancellation

For orders with multiple items, you can cancel individual items before shipment via **My Orders** → **Manage Items**.

## Seller-Initiated Cancellation

If a seller cancels your order (out-of-stock, pricing error):

- You receive full refund automatically within 3–5 business days.
- A ₹50 coupon is issued as compensation for the inconvenience.

## Flash Sale / Pre-Order Cancellation

Items purchased during flash sales or pre-orders follow the same cancellation policy, **except** that pre-order deposits are non-refundable if cancelled within 48 hours of the dispatch date.
